FMEasySync: Acknowledgements

Thanks to the following people who have helped make EasySync what it is today...

Kirk Bowman of MightyData and Art of Value
Kirk gave some terrific early feedback and guidance, and saw EasySync's potential when it was still in a primordial stage. Thanks Kirk, for sharing your time and your wisdom!

Paul Jansen of APJ Computing Solutions
and Crispin Hodges of MoleSoft
Paul and Crispin have provided invaluable feedback throughout the EasySync beta test. They've pushed EasySync to the limits and beyond. Thanks guys! You've helped to make EasySync a better sync framework! Thanks also go to Paul for helping me work through the dreaded "round trip" issue.

Hal Gumbert of CampSoftware
Hal provided some very encouraging feedback, and is currently working on a way to add field-level sync to EasySync. Thanks Hal! We're looking forward to seeing what you've come up with.

Darren Burgess of MightyData
Darren took some time to review EasySync and ask some tough questions - questions that have helped improve EasySync. Thanks Darren!

Bill Heizer of FileMaker, Inc.
Bill took the time to answer some "interesting" questions that I ran past him. Some of them were, admittedly, a little odd. Regardless, Bill was gracious enough to provide the answers. Bill was also instrumental in making support for deletions more "FileMaker-like," and his expertise in deployment helped bring life to EasyDeploy. Thanks Bill! You rock!

Stephen Dolenski of Ocean West, Inc.
Stephen has graciously set us up with our own forum over on FMForums. This will be a great way to provide support and spread the word about EasySync. Thanks Stephen!

And thanks to all of our Beta Testers!
Your feedback, ideas, and suggestions have helped make EasySync what it is today. Thank you!
